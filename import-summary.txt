Moved Files:
------------

Eclipse projects. Here's how the projects were restructured:

* libs\commons-net-3.5.jar => app\libs\commons-net-3.5.jar
* libs\PNGDecoder.jar => app\libs\PNGDecoder.jar
* src\ => app\src\main\java\
